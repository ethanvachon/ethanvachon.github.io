About me

My entire life I've had a passion for solving puzzles, tech, and most importantly learning. When I was young I was learning to code, even going to coding summer camps and just learning on my own. I eventually found out how much I enjoy chess, and I started competing locally in the highest available division. Eventually, I found my next passion, which was music. I joined the band and wanted to be like the seniors I looked up to, competing in the state solo competition. Two years later, I placed 1st at that competition and soon after felt as though my time with music had come to a natural end, so I started my search for something I'm passionate enough about to make my career. I started learning to code on my own on CodeCademy and fell in love with it. I soon after went though the immersive full stack course at Boise Codeworks and never looked back. I have a love for coding and working with anyone else that shares that passion.
